The evaluation of this course unit is carried out through the writing of the introduction and the state of the art (or related works) of the dissertation.

In the introduction, it should be clear what the topic under investigation in the dissertation is that motivated the research presented in the state of the art.

The document to be submitted may be in the final format of the dissertation. In practice, it will consist of two of your normal chapters: the Introduction and the State of the Art.

The document must be submitted by January 6 for evaluation in the Regular Period, or by January 22 for evaluation in the Resit Period. Improvements are still allowed according to the Study Regulations.

The document will be evaluated by a group of faculty members and the dissertation supervisors.
